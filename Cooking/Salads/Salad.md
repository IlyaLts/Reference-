## Ingredients

- Tomatoes *(three)*
- Cucumber
- Dills *(Three or four)*
- Green onions *(Four)*
- Mayonnaise
- Salt 
- Bread *(optional)*

## Instructions

1. Cut a cucumber.
1. Cut tomatoes.
1. Cut green onions.
1. Cut Dills.
1. Mix everything.
1. Salt to taste.
1. Add Mayonnaise to that and mix everything again.
